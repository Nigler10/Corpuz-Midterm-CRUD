Who is Anna Yanami?



Anna Yanami (八奈見杏菜, Yanami Anna) is one of the Main Characters of Too Many Losing Heroines series as well as one of the titular Losing Heroines. She's a First Year Student of Class 1-C of Tsuwabuki High School and also a member of the literature club. She has romantic feelings for her Childhood Friend and Classmate Sosuke Hakamada and best friend of his girl friend Karen Himemiya.
